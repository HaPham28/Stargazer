About Stargazer

Our Mission

Stargazing is a fun, interesting passion for many people. However, there are many factors that can affect the quality of a stargazing experience. Among these are light pollution, weather, and location. It is our goal to provide our users with a single platform that can be used to plan for and overcome all of these hurles in order to achieve an optimal viewing experience.

How To Use Stargazer

To use the tool, simply start typing your location in the search box on the home page. From the options that come up, select the most appropriate choice, and the application will begin a search. After the search is complete, recommended locations will be fetched along with weather information. The best location can be selected based on the reviews and light pollution levels, and the best time to go stargazing can be found from the weather location. To make this easier, each day of the week is rated for how favorable the conditions are for stargazing.

Some additional general stargazing information, including visible constellations and astronomical events are also shown as additional information.

How It Works

To provide you with all of the information needed to plan your next stargazing trip, Stargazer integrates with a variety data sources. these sources include Aeris Weather, Google Places, lookUP, and a private light pollution API. All of this data is brought together in order to help users answer the questions of when and where to stargaze!

We would like to give special thanks to Jurij Stare for access to the light pollution data and light pollution heatmap.
